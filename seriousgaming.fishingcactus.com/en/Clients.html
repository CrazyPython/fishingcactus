R.O.G.E.R

Fishing Cactus worked hand in glove with two neuro psychological practitioners from the Erasmus hospital in Brussels to produce a therapeutic serious game called R.O.G.E.R. The software backs neuro psychologists while testing potentially brain-damaged patients. It operates on Xbox 360 or PC through Kinect, displaying a full 3D environment during the test.
(Hospital Erasmus ULB)
